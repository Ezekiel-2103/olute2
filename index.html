ABOUT US

Who We Are Olute Football Club was formed in January 2021 to discover young soccer talents in Nigeria and the rest of Africa to make them better persons in the society. We believe in the numerous human and natural resources that Nigeria and Africa possess, most especially in the football industry, and Olute Football Club was set up to recognize, develop and deploy these talents.

OUR MISSION

Our aim is to produce the next generation of big stars and talents in the world of football and give them the best education in the game that will last them for the rest of their lives.

OUR STORY

Olute Football Association was founded in 2021 by Audu Boma Aliu who hails from Kogi State. Boma shared a great passion for football. Boma always strives to impact more than just the players, but the surrounding community at large. That has been the driving force of Olute Football Association since the beginning.

Audu Boma Aliu

FOUNDER

A CLUB FOR THE COMMUNITY

Olute Football Association aim to be more than a football club. For so many different reasons, young talented players have refused to play. We hope to support young talents both male and female, provide basic necessities of life, provide football kits and adequate training facilities and camping for them to play. We are always looking to do our part in the community to support youth development and local businesses.
